Instruction vs Dialog

Date: October 29, 2023

Ever noticed the difference between instruction and dialogue? 🤔 It’s like being given a map 🗺️ vs. exploring the route together! 🛤️

When dialogue takes a backseat, creativity often hides in the shadows. 🙊 Why? Because a one-way street of just instructions might snuff out morale, and folks might pocket those sparkling ideas 💡 instead of sharing them.

Ever faced a policy change that felt... off? It’s usually not about what’s being said, but HOW it’s being delivered. Instruction-only changes? More like resistance magnets! 🧲

Remember, instructional roads often lead to dead-ends. 🚫 Creating a feedback loop, however, is like having GPS for success! 🛰️

Let’s make sure we’re not just instructing, but also engaging in rich conversations. Your team's next big idea might just be a dialogue away! 🎉
